من نحن About KATO

كاتو مش مجرد صيانة — ده نظام تشغيل KATO is more than maintenance — it's an operating system

KATO is a service center specialized in maintenance and contracting for factories and companies. We provide integrated facility management, operation, and maintenance within industrial sites with a preventive mindset and an organized team.

Our goal is not just to fix a failure, but to prevent it in the first place. Through a clear maintenance plan, organized execution, and technical reports that help management make the right decision at the right time.
